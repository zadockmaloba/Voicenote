Welcome to Voicenote

Voicenote is a platform where students request for tailor-made videos on concepts they need more understanding. Voicenote is also a platform created for Professional tutors; where the tutors from a particular institution create their own portal, upload videos then invite learners of that institution via a specific link to access the videos; and if a student from another institution wants to access the videos from that tutor, they will have to before being given access.
